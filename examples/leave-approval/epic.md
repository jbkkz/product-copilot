# Epic: Digital Leave Approval Workflow

> Delivery epic — generated by Product Copilot

**Milestone:** Pilot

**Goal:** Replace the email/Excel leave approval process with a configurable, auditable digital workflow that enforces balance checks, routes approvals and notifications by role, and supports a pilot-then-scale rollout.

**Business value:** Eliminates inconsistent, untracked approvals and manual balance errors, giving HR a full audit trail and faster escalations while letting each client run its own approval rules without custom builds.

## In scope

- Leave request lifecycle: submission, manager approval, optional HR approval above threshold, rejection
- Real-time balance/quota validation before approval
- Configurable multi-level approval circuit per client/country/contract
- Delegation layer overlaying existing permissions
- Auto-escalation after 5 business days of inactivity
- HR-validated cancellation flow, including already-started leave
- Email and in-app notifications on key workflow events
- One-way calendar sync (system to calendar)
- Multi-tenant configuration isolation to support pilot-first rollout
- Audit trail of requests and entitlement rule changes

## Out of scope

- Payroll integration
- Migration of historical/past leave requests into the new audit trail
- Reporting dashboards and exports
- Country templates beyond France for this phase
- Two-way calendar sync
