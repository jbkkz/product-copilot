    ✓ Auto-escalation after 5 business days of inaction
    ✓ HR is sole editor of approved requests and sole validator of

  Cost driver   the configurable approval-circuit and entitlement rule engine

MAIN RISKS
  ⚠ The approval circuit is shared platform infrastructure — changes here likely
    ripple into other modules that use the same permission/approval framework.
  ⚠ Overlapping leave requests revalidated at approval time introduce a real
    concurrency hazard on balance calculation.
  ⚠ The escalation timer and a human approver/delegate acting near-
    simultaneously can produce a race with no defined tie-breaker yet.
  ⚠ Not migrating historical requests weakens the audit trail the client is
    presumably building this system for.
  ⚠ HR data falls under GDPR — the audit log itself will need access controls,
    not just the workflow.

OPPORTUNITIES
  High leverage
    ◆ Generalize the configurable approval circuit so other workflows (contract
      sign-off, invoice validation) can reuse it rather than building bespoke
      circuits later.
  Medium leverage
    ◆ Formalize the delegation layer as a reusable permission overlay rather
      than a leave-specific patch.
  Future idea
    ◆ Extract the balance/entitlement engine as a standalone service other HR
      features could query.
