# Digital Leave Approval Workflow — v1.0

> Release notes — generated by Product Copilot

Requesting and approving time off no longer means emails and spreadsheets. Employees can submit leave requests that are automatically checked against their balance and routed to the right approver, with full visibility into where each request stands. Managers and HR get a consistent, auditable process that adapts to each client's own rules.

## What's new

- Submit a leave request in a few clicks, with balance automatically checked before approval
- Requests route automatically to the manager, and to HR when needed above a set threshold
- Requests that sit too long escalate automatically after 5 business days so nothing stalls
- Delegate approval authority when a manager is unavailable, without losing track of who approved what
- Cancel a request — even leave that has already started — through an HR-validated process
- Get notified by email and in-app at every key step, and see approved leave reflected in your calendar
- Approval rules can be tailored per client, country or contract, supporting a pilot-first rollout before wider scale-up

## Not included yet

- Reporting and dashboards for leave data are not yet included in this release
- Detailed test scenarios for concurrent approvals and precise escalation timing are still being finalised
- Historical leave requests will not be migrated into the new audit trail
- Changes to entitlement rule templates themselves are not yet tracked in the audit history
- Calendar sync is one-way (into the calendar); it does not read changes back from the calendar
- No payroll system integration at this stage

## Before you start

- Because approval permissions are shared across other modules on the platform, rolling this out may touch screens beyond leave management — worth reviewing with your team before go-live
- A pilot rollout is recommended first, with per-client configuration kept isolated, before scaling to all clients
- Delivery timeline still needs to be confirmed
